About Us

Our History

The Sparks Foundation Bank (TSF) is an Indian multinational, public sector banking and financial services statutory body headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra. SBI is the 43rd largest bank in the world and ranked 231st in the Fortune Global 500 list of the world's biggest corporations of 2020. A nationalised bank, it is the largest in India with a 20% market share by assets and a 23% share of the total loan and deposits market.

TSF provides a range of banking products through its network of branches in India and overseas, including products aimed at non-resident Indians (NRIs). TSF has 17 regional hubs and 55 zonal offices that are located at important cities throughout India.
